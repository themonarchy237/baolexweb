Our Story

At BAOLEX, everything started with a simple but powerful idea: using artificial intelligence to help entrepreneurs save time, automate their tasks, and grow their businesses faster. Founded by a young tech enthusiast with a passion for innovation, BAOLEX was born from a clear vision: to make automation tools accessible to everyone, even with a small budget. In a world where every second counts, we decided to create smart, fast, and personalized solutions. Today, BAOLEX supports creators, small business owners, influencers, and startups by automating their sales, customer service, social media, and more. With our expertise in chatbots, automation, design, and AI, we turn simple ideas into powerful systems.
